# Survey Request Page - Quick Reference

## At a Glance

Track and manage survey and feedback requests from companies during your job search.

## Core Actions

- **Add Survey Request**: Track new survey/feedback requests
- **Update Status**: Quick status changes from card view
- **Set Priorities**: Mark urgent or important surveys
- **Open Surveys**: One-click access to survey URLs
- **Link to Applications**: Connect surveys to job applications

## Survey Types

- Post-Interview Feedback
- Candidate Experience
- Compensation Survey
- Company Culture
- Glassdoor/Indeed/LinkedIn Reviews
- Referral Programs
- Exit Surveys
- Market Research
